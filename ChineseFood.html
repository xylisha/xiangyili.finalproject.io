3 Traditional Chinese Food You Must Eat in Chinese



1. Hot Pot

Hot pot is one of China's most famous dish. It is a representation of 团圆（reunion) in Chinese culture. Family and friends gather at a round table and enjoy a food dippings together.



2. Pecking Duck

If you visit China, you don't want to miss out of Peking Duck. It is a specially cooked duck dish that has the most marvalous texture. People usually eat it with Chinese pancake, cucumber, green oinion and this delicious secret sauce.



3. Stinky Tofu

Traveling means exploring, right? If you are felling extra adventurous today, you should try this famous Chinaese dish: Stinky Tofu. Just like its name, it does not smell the best.
